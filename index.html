Play as you Learn

We at “KiddyPi”, have a vision to engage and ignite children’s mind by providing an interactive and enjoyable approach to STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) Education. Through the cultivation of computational thinking skills, we emphasize the practical applications of problem-solving, enabling children to establish meaningful connections between their education, future employment, and community engagement. By nurturing a joyful and purposeful learning environment, we aspire to empower kids to identify their preferred disciplines and pursue careers that offer both satisfaction and fulfillment.

What is STEM?

Where Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics come together and open up a path towards creation and innovation.

Why STEM?

Children are immersed in and dependent on technology, often lacking a comprehensive understanding of its inner workings, leaving them unprepared to survive in a technology-centric society.

KiddyPi Approach

KiddyPi takes a practical and immersive approach to STEM Education, where theoretical concepts are brought to life through hands-on projects in a smart, enjoyable, and friendly environment.

KiddyPi

KiddyPi is a dynamic after-school programming club designed to empower children in their journey to become adventurous Science Superheroes at school, evolve as geeky Junior Scientists in college, and ultimately flourish as influential STEM Leaders in their careers. Our program centers around imparting computational thinking skills to children, encouraging them to apply problem-solving techniques in real-world contexts, and fostering meaningful connections between school, work, and community.

Online Classes

KiddyPi offers online classes for children to learn various skills in the comfort of their homes. The courses cover Programming, App development, Web development, Game development, 3D Design, Raspberry Pi, Robotics, and Electronics. Dedicated mentors provide high-quality training, and guide the students at their own pace, ensuring an engaging learning experience.

After School Workshops

Programs are offered after school hours to help children with valuable supplementary learning opportunities. We embrace a hands-on teaching methodology, inspiring children to engage in creative, interactive, and stimulating projects.

Summer Camps

Block-Based Programming

Block-based programming is a type of programming approach wherein instead of manually writing lines of code, the programmers assemble together visual blocks that represent different code elements. We teach block-based programming using Scratch, a beginner-friendly visual programming language.

Mobile app development

We believe that teaching mobile app development to children can help them develop essential skills like problem-solving, creativity, and logical thinking, while also preparing them for the demands of the modern world. We teach mobile app development using the MIT App Inventor platform. 

Web development

Web development is an important skill in today’s digital age, as it allows businesses and individuals to showcase their products, services, and ideas on a global platform. We teach web development using various programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. 

Game development

Game development is the process of creating video games, mobile games, and other interactive games using various programming languages and software development tools. We teach game development using Scratch, Python, and MIT App Inventor.

3D Designing & Modeling

3D designing and modeling is the process of creating three-dimensional objects or environments using specialized software tools. We teach 3D design using tools like TinkerCAD. 

Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pi is a small, affordable computer that can be used for a variety of projects and applications. Raspberry Pi is a versatile tool that can be used to teach children about programming, electronics, and robotics.

Robotics

Robotics involves the study and creation of robots-machines that can perform tasks automatically or autonomously.  The study of robotics involves designing, building, programming, electronics, and testing robots. At our center, we guide children in the complete process of creating a robot to address real-world challenges, from its inception to practical implementation.
